SYMPTOMS OF COVID 19



          MOST COMMON SYMPTOMS -



1.Fever

A temporary increase in
temperature above 100.4 F
or 38 C due to an illness.









2.Dry Cough

A sudden, forceful hacking sound to
release air and clear an irritation
in the dry throat or airway.









3.Tiredness

     Low energy and a strong desire
      to sleep that interferes
      with normal daily activities.






LESS COMMON SYMPTOMS -








SERIOUS SYMPTOMS -




1. Dificulty or Shortness in breathing-

Shortness of
breathe
is a feeling
of not being able
to breathe
well enough.






2. Chest pain-

Discomfort in the chest including a dull ache,
a crushing or burning feeling, a sharp stabbing pain
and pain that radiates to the neck or shoulder.






Seek immediate medical attention if
serious symptoms are noticed.
